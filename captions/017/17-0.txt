 This module examines some of the complicated dilemmas associated with counter-terrorism measures implemented by the United States since 9/11. It consists of two segments. The first segment discusses how the attacks on 9/11 contributed to the decisions by the American government to undertake wars in both Afghanistan and Iraq. The second segment defines torture and discusses a couple videos of that highlight the moral and ethical dilemmas posed by incorporating torture as a tool of national policy. The conversation will continue in the live portion of the class which reviews the debate over the ethics of torture found Meierfeld and Krauthammer readings that were assigned for this module.
